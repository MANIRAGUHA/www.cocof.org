Empowering rural women

COCOF

- -

The Advisory Council on Women (Conseil Consultatif des Femmes, “COCOF”) is a women's organization of Rwandan established under law in December 1994

Main objectif

To motivate the Rwandan women to not place themselves outside of the politics of their country by issuing their views and opinions, and actively participating in the conduct of public affairs;

MISSION

OCOF's mission is to ensure the socio-economic and political development of women to have the capacity to meet their needs and safeguard their rights.

VISION

COCOF wants to promote self-sufficiency and satisfaction of socio-economic and political needs of women and fight for their rights. Its vision is as follows: "A society where women are complementary to man, fully enjoying the same rights as men and not dependent on the latter. Thus, the woman is a partner of a man, capable of self-sufficiency, and capable to meet its socio-economic and political needs and fight for the respect of its rights."
