COCOF

Conseil Consultatif des Femmes

The Advisory Council on Women (Conseil Consultatif des Femmes, “COCOF”) is a women's organization of Rwandan established under law in December 1994 by a group of peasant women from the former Commune of Musumbira (current Kamonyi District). The aim of this organization was to improve the socio-economic development of rural women and safeguard their rights. At its inception, COCOF counted 77 women. Currently, it includes 545 members exclusively females, and 4,281 beneficiaries both sexes, with 75% females. All the members were grouped into 15 cooperatives.
